About me

I thought it would be fun to actually create a web page tracking my 100 days of code, with an added dose of commitment that goes with it. I started coding in May last year having followed some HTML, CSS and Vanilla Javascript courses. I now feel its time to consolidate that learning before I move onto JS frameworks and backend so plan to take the next few months getting to grips with the basics.

For the first half I intend to do lots of small projects, either challenges or short tutorial projects to help me build up the structure for building projects. Once I feel comfortable at this I will move on to creating my own projects and websites. I will use a mix of youtube and frontendmentor challenges to prepare me for the 2nd half of this challenge.

All of this will be recorded day by day below with a new card for each day. This website will also adapt as it goes along and I learn how to add new features and will act as my first project of the #100daysofcode.
